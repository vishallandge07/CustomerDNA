# CustomerDNA
● Problem Statement : 
Modern e-commerce platforms collect vast amounts of customer data, yet many still rely on generic marketing strategies that fail to address the unique purchasing behaviors of different customers. This leads to inefficient marketing campaigns, reduced customer engagement, missed opportunities to retain high-value customers, and difficulty identifying customers who are at risk of churning.

● Solution : 
The SmartCart Clustering System is an AI-powered customer segmentation solution that leverages unsupervised machine learning to identify hidden patterns in customer behavior. By analyzing customer demographics, purchasing habits, website activity, and engagement metrics, the system groups customers into meaningful clusters based on their similarities
